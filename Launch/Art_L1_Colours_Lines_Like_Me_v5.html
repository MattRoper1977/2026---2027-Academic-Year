👩‍🏫 TA Focus — your job, stage by stage

Set up & safetyAprons on, water nearby, surfaces covered. Keep paint stations spaced. Watch for sensory overwhelm — offer a quieter spot if needed.
Warm-up (colours)Let the pupil lead colour choices. Echo their words: "You picked teal — it feels calm to you." No right answers.
Artist (Part B)Show the image. Ask them to point, not write. Capture one photo + their reaction. That's the Part B entry done.
Making (Part A)Model the technique first, then hands off. Photograph the doing, not just the finished piece. Get a 10-sec video if you can.
Name the techniqueBefore tidy-up, prompt: "What did you use?" Capture the word — this is the rung toward Arts Award Explore.
Consent & filingNo face-consent → hands/work only. Drop straight into the pupil's Discover folder so the logbook fills itself.
